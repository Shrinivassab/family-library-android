Privacy Policy

Family Library AI Companion

Last updated: July 2026

Family Library AI Companion ("the App") is a free educational app that helps parents create personalized reading plans and learning activities for their children. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App does and does not collect, and how it is used.

Free App, No Account Required

The App is completely free to use. It does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any registration details to use its features.

No Collection, Storage, or Sale of Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information about you or your child. Any details you enter in the App, such as your child's name, age, class, or preferred language, are kept only on your own device and are never transmitted to us or to any third party for storage.

Uploaded Book Photos and PDFs

When you upload a photo of a book page or a PDF, that content is sent to third-party AI services — Google's Gemma API and the Groq API — solely for the purpose of generating a reading plan, questions, or related learning content based on that material.

  • These uploads are used only to generate the requested content.
  • We do not store copies of the uploaded photos or PDFs on our servers.
  • We do not use this content for any purpose other than generating the learning material you requested.

No Data Shared for Advertising

We do not share, sell, or use any personal data for advertising purposes. The App does not display third-party ads and does not use your data or your child's data to build advertising profiles.

Intended Use and Parental Guidance

The App is designed to be used by children under the guidance and supervision of a parent or guardian. Parents are responsible for entering their child's details and for supervising the App's use.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly.
